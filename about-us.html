About Us

We're a brand new Australian company with a vision to create high-quality tools & content which are accessible to all Australian Home Buyers


We aim to help Australians make the best property decision for them without bias. Taking the power of knowledge away from those who know the jargon and dark magic of property and into everyday Australians.


Our aspirations are lofty and the sky is the limit. While concurrently, if Habitat provides a clearer picture of the property world for just one Australian, we've made an impact. And that's what we're all about.
